Welcome to our Café

    Our Philosophy

    Coffee beans are a gift. They are the foundation of our café's culture, an evolution of taste, the crafting of hot brewed goodness. To brew is an artform, nearly comparable to zen meditation, without haste, the grinding fills the presence - there is no goal, only perfection in the moment. Some say, there is no perfection, only excellence by striving forever. The lowtech basics are key: mechanically ground beans, a french press and patience are our recipe.

    No sugar, real milk. While detoxing from sugar addiction it became clear to our barista team that milk all by itself is sweet enough for our taste!
